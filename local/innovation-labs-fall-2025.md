# Fall 2025 Innovation Labs Recap

Our first Innovation Labs event brought together 40 students and 8 sponsor engineers for a weekend of applied AI building.

## What Happened

Teams of 4-5 students each tackled a real problem proposed by our sponsor partners. Over 48 hours, they scoped, built, and presented working prototypes.

## Projects

### Team Alpha — Supply Chain Anomaly Detection
Built a real-time anomaly detection pipeline using streaming sensor data. Used Python, FastAPI, and a lightweight transformer model to flag unusual patterns in warehouse logistics.

### Team Beta — Resume-to-Role Matching
Created an NLP pipeline that matches student resumes to open roles at sponsor companies. Used sentence embeddings and cosine similarity to rank fit.

### Team Gamma — Meeting Summarizer
Built a Whisper-based transcription tool that generates structured meeting notes with action items. Deployed as a Slack bot for our sponsor's engineering team.

## Outcomes

- **3 projects** continued as semester-long capstones
- **2 students** received internship offers directly from the event
- **1 project** was adopted internally by a sponsor company
